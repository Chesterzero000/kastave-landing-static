Terms

These terms explain how the Kastave reservation works before final product purchase opens.

$1 reservation

The $1 reservation gives you early access to Kastave launch updates and eligibility for a 25% discount toward the planned $1299 final product price when final purchase opens.

Not a final product purchase

The reservation is not the final purchase of hardware and does not automatically charge the remaining product price. You will receive launch information before final checkout and can decide whether to complete the order.

Discount eligibility

The 25% discount is intended for the reserved customer and may be subject to launch timing, shipping availability, payment completion and final checkout terms. It has no cash value.

Kastave is currently being validated through field testing and production planning. Final specifications, availability, shipping regions, taxes and support terms may be updated before launch.
